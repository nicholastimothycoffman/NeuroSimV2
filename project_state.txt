Project

NeuroSimV2

Mission

Build a production-quality cognitive knowledge graph platform that

and introductory AI engineering.

Produce a polished Version 1.0 suitable for job applications,
then continue expanding the platform after employment.

## PROJECT PHILOSOPHY

The objective is not to build every possible feature before applying
for positions.

The objective is to complete a polished Version 1.0 that clearly
demonstrates modern backend, cloud, and AI engineering skills.

Future releases will continue to evolve the platform after Version 1.0
is complete.
